# Decision History

## Principle

> Good documentation preserves decisions.

## Meaning

The value of documentation is not only to record information.

Its deeper value is to preserve:

- why something was decided
- who decided it
- what alternatives were considered
- what context existed at the time
- what the decision affects

## Analogy

Git commit history is useful not only because it shows what changed.

It is useful because it helps us understand why the change happened.
