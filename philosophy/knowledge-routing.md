# Knowledge Routing

## Principle

> Knowledge should be routed, not broadcast.

## Meaning

Information is not valuable simply because it exists.

It becomes valuable when the right context reaches the right person at the right time.

## Why It Matters

In many teams, documents are broadcast to everyone.

The result is cognitive overload.

A better system should route knowledge according to:

- role
- task
- decision point
- timing
- required level of detail

## Product Implication

An AI agent should not summarize everything for everyone.

It should determine who needs what next.
