# Case Study — Portal Device Ticket

## Source

A Trello card describing Portal administrators viewing a full-platform device list.

## Progress Observed

Compared with earlier documents, this version includes:

- demand background
- purpose
- responsibility boundaries
- what Portal admins can and cannot do
- device categories
- acceptance criteria

## Remaining Issue

It is still too long to function as an engineering ticket.

The content is better structured, but not yet optimized for reader priority.

## Product Lesson

Distillation is not only about reducing length.

It is about prioritizing what each reader needs first.
