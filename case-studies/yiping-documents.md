# Case Study — Yiping Documents

## Original Observation

Yiping's documents were long, detailed, and generated in a discussion-oriented style.

They were not necessarily bad documents.

They were simply not suitable for engineers as ticket-level implementation guidance.

## Problem

The same content was given to every audience.

This caused cognitive overload.

## Improvement Direction

Turn long discussion documents into role-specific outputs:

- discussion guide for stakeholders
- specification for SA
- executable ticket for engineers
- acceptance criteria for QA

## Product Lesson

The issue is not document quality alone.

The issue is audience mismatch.
