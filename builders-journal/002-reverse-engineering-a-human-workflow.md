# Builder's Journal 002 — Reverse Engineering a Human Workflow

## Key Realization

We were not simply designing an AI agent.

We were reverse engineering a human workflow.

The question was not:

> What would Sherry say?

The question was:

> Why does Sherry think this way?

## Why This Matters

If the product only imitates a person, it becomes a personality clone.

That is not the goal.

The goal is to understand repeatable workflow patterns:

- how context is selected
- how decisions are preserved
- how information is routed
- how role-specific outputs are created
- how cognitive load is reduced

## Working Principle

> Every useful AI agent begins with understanding a human workflow, not a model architecture.

## Important Boundary

This is not “Sherry GPT.”

Sherry is the first case study.

The real product direction is workflow abstraction.
