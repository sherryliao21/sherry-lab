# Builder's Journal 005 — Not Building Sherry GPT

## Temptation

At one point, the project sounded like:

> We are building Sherry.

That was a powerful sentence, but also dangerous.

## Correction

The goal is not to build a personality clone.

The goal is not to ask:

> How would Sherry answer?

The better question is:

> How should this knowledge flow?

## Refined Direction

Sherry is not the product.

Sherry is the first case study.

The real work is extracting a repeatable workflow from a real knowledge worker.

## Product Boundary

Not:

- Sherry GPT
- personality replication
- imitation

Instead:

- workflow abstraction
- knowledge routing
- context design
- decision support
