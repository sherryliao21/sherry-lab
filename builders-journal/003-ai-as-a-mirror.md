# Builder's Journal 003 — AI as a Mirror

## Background

I used to be afraid of AI.

I believed that my value as an engineer came from writing code. When AI became capable of writing code, I thought my value would disappear.

## What Changed

After learning more about AI tools, agent concepts, skills, workflows, and AI-powered IDEs, I realized something unexpected.

AI can replace parts of coding.

But it did not replace:

- judgment
- reasoning
- architectural thinking
- workflow design
- knowledge distillation
- deciding what information should go to whom

## Core Reflection

> I thought AI would replace my skills. Instead, it revealed which of my skills were truly irreplaceable.

## Why This Matters

This Capstone is not only about building an AI agent.

It is also about redefining my own professional value.
